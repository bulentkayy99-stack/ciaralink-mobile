Built for Australian NDIS providers

Purpose-built for NDIS care — and serious about your data.

CiaraLink is built for Australian NDIS and healthcare teams — with row-level security and consent-first access designed in, so the right people see the right data, and the participant stays in control of their own information. CiaraLink provides software tools; your organisation remains responsible for NDIS registration, care delivery, and regulatory compliance.

Row-level security

Every record is access-controlled at the database — users only ever load their own data.

Consent-first

Sharing is governed by recorded consent — revocable, and visible to the participant.

Encrypted & private

Your data is encrypted and access-controlled — never sold, never mined for ads.

Australian-built for the NDIS

Built for Australian care teams and privacy expectations — workflows aligned with how NDIS providers work, not adapted from overseas.

Platform vs provider. CiaraLink operates the software platform (hosting, security, product support). We are not an NDIS registered provider, auditor, or compliance authority. Provider organisations using CiaraLink remain responsible for registration, worker screening, participant consent, payroll, NDIA claims, and SIRS reporting. Payroll, claiming, and incident tools are decision-support aids — verify outputs with qualified professionals before relying on them.
