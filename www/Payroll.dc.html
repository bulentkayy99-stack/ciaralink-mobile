Payroll — SCHADS interpretation

Compute itemised, audit-defensible gross pay from shifts.
⚠️ Decision-support only — not certified payroll. Rates require verification. This tool interprets the Social, Community, Home Care and Disability Services Industry Award (MA000100). The award config shipped with this build uses PLACEHOLDER rates, multipliers, spans and allowances — they are illustrative, not the current legal SCHADS figures. Every figure must be confirmed against the current award (and your state public-holiday calendar / any enterprise agreement) before paying anyone. Breakdowns computed from placeholder config are flagged and must not be treated as payable.
